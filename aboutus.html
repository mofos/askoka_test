ASOKA® Trademark was created by Late. Sri. D.V. Sainath Gupta in 1934 as a revered respect to the great monarch, King Asoka. Vijaya Chemicals & Toilet and works manufactured cosmetics based on natural ingredients and is the first Company to introduce ASOKA® Amla Hair Oil, ASOKA® Brahmi Hair Oil, ASOKA® Vepa Hair Oil & others. The products enjoyed by the customers confidence in South India and other foreign countries like Sri Lanka & Malaysia.
